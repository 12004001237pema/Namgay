Another highlight while touring the Thimphu Valley is the National Memorial Chorten which is another visible landmark of the region. Built in 1974 in memoriam to the third king of Bhutan, this Tibetan style stupa is a place of worship for many Bhutanese.


Tourists are welcome, however, in the evening it is discouraged as many worshipers would like this time to practice their faith without any onlookers. Regardless when you are there, it is a memorable experience to be in a place that evokes such an ambiance of utter peace and tranquillity.
